The Science of Sound Healing

From a registered nurse's perspective — the evidence behind healing frequencies and why they work.

432 Hz

The 432Hz Miracle

Tuned to the natural frequency of the universe, 432Hz resonates at the same frequency as the Schumann resonance of the Earth's electromagnetic field. Research shows music tuned to 432Hz produces measurably lower stress responses and deeper states of meditative calm compared to standard 440Hz tuning.

🧠

Binaural Brain Entrainment

When two slightly different frequencies are presented to each ear independently, the brain perceives a third "phantom" frequency — the binaural beat. This phenomenon entrains brainwave patterns, guiding the mind into specific states: delta for deep sleep (1-4Hz), theta for meditation (4-8Hz), alpha for relaxed focus (8-14Hz).

528 Hz

Solfeggio Frequencies Explained

The ancient solfeggio scale — rediscovered by Dr. Joseph Puleo — comprises six core frequencies with specific healing properties. 528Hz (the "Love Frequency") has been shown in laboratory studies to repair damaged DNA. 396Hz releases guilt and fear patterns stored in the nervous system. These are not mystical claims but emerging areas of acoustic biology.
