T. Lott's Picks

Recommended Reading

Beyond T. Lott's own works, these titles have shaped the Sacred Cycles philosophy and are recommended for every healing journey.

📖

The Body Keeps the Score

By Bessel van der Kolk, M.D.

The landmark clinical study on how trauma lives in the body — and the healing modalities (including yoga, EMDR, and mindfulness) that free it. Required reading for anyone on a healing journey.

🕊️

You Can Heal Your Life

By Louise Hay

A timeless masterpiece connecting thought patterns to physical well-being. Louise Hay's affirmations and mind-body philosophy beautifully complement the Sacred Cycles framework of holistic renewal.

✨

The Power of Now

By Eckhart Tolle

Eckhart Tolle's spiritual classic on present-moment awareness and ego dissolution. A foundational text for anyone beginning their journey into mindfulness, breathwork, and conscious healing.
