Integrated Healing Modalities

T. Lott, RN is a Registered Nurse with over 18 years of dedicated psychiatric nursing experience. Having spent nearly two decades serving patients on the front lines of mental health care — many of whom couldn't afford the help they desperately needed — T. Lott witnessed firsthand the gaps between clinical treatment and true holistic healing.

Sacred Cycles Renewal was born from this profound understanding: that real transformation requires more than a prescription. It requires an integration of mind, body, and spirit — grounded in clinical knowledge yet elevated by ancient healing wisdom.

"After 18 years as a psychiatric nurse serving those who couldn't afford help, I developed Sacred Cycles to bring clinical-grade healing techniques to everyone ready to invest in their transformation."
— T. Lott, RN
